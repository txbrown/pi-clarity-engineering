# Spike / Research Ticket Example

## Ticket type
Spike / Research

## Problem
The team is unsure whether local-first draft storage should use IndexedDB directly or a wrapper library.

## Why
Choosing the wrong storage approach could increase maintenance cost or limit offline behavior.

## Scope
- Compare two to three viable approaches.
- Prototype the riskiest operation if needed.
- Recommend one approach with evidence.

## Non-goals
- Building production draft storage.
- Migrating existing data.

## Acceptance criteria
- Decision criteria are documented.
- Options are compared against the criteria.
- Recommendation includes trade-offs, risks, and next implementation ticket suggestion.

## References
- Browser storage requirements.
- Offline draft behavior notes.

## Open questions
- Required browser support floor?

## Validation
Answer/evidence/recommendation delivered within the timebox.

## Timebox
One day.
