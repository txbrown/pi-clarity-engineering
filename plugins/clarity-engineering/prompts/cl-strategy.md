Apply Clarity Strategy to the input below.

Strategy is upstream of Clarity Engineering. It decides whether an idea, opportunity, product bet, engineering investment, or founder instinct is worth shaping. It is not a delivery lifecycle stage and does not change the lifecycle:

```text
Shape → Plan → Build → Review → Compound
```

Evaluate:

- strategic thesis;
- pain/value intensity;
- product or engineering story;
- why now / readiness;
- options and trade-offs;
- smallest tangible learning path;
- kill or park criteria.

Return a recommendation: `shape`, `spike`, `prototype`, `RFC`, `park`, or `kill`. If the recommendation is `shape`, hand off to Shape rather than planning implementation details. Ask one focused question only when strategic judgement requires human input.

$ARGUMENTS
